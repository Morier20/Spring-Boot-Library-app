Welcome to City Library,

your community hub for knowledge, discovery, and connection. Established in 1987, we've been serving our community for over 35 years with a commitment to providing accessible information and resources for all. Our Mission At City Library, we believe that access to information is a fundamental right. Our mission is to inspire lifelong learning, advance knowledge, and strengthen our community by providing free access to information, resources, and experiences.
